# AI Appointment Booking Bot

## Pages & Features

### 1. Dashboard
- Total bookings today / this week / this month
- Upcoming appointments timeline
- Cancellation & no-show rate
- Bot conversation success rate

### 2. Booking Flow Builder
- Visual conversation flow editor
- Service selection step configuration
- Date & time picker with availability sync
- Customer information collection fields
- Confirmation & follow-up message templates

### 3. Calendar & Availability
- Staff / resource calendar view (day / week / month)
- Define available slots per service & staff member
- Buffer time between appointments
- Blackout dates & holidays
- Multi-location support

### 4. Services
- Service catalog (name, duration, price, description)
- Group services by category
- Service-specific booking rules
- Add-on & upsell options

### 5. Customer Management
- Customer directory with booking history
- Loyalty & visit tracking
- Preferred staff & time preferences
- Contact info & notes

### 6. Notifications & Reminders
- Automated booking confirmation (email / SMS / WhatsApp)
- Reminder schedule (24h, 1h before)
- Cancellation & reschedule notifications
- Follow-up & review request messages

### 7. Channel Deployment
- Website chat widget
- WhatsApp & Telegram bot
- Instagram DM integration
- Embed booking link on any page

### 8. Analytics
- Booking volume trends
- Popular services & time slots
- No-show & cancellation analysis
- Revenue per service / staff
- Export as PDF / CSV

### 9. Settings
- Business hours & timezone
- Booking policies (cancellation window, max advance)
- Payment integration (deposit / full payment)
- Staff roles & booking permissions
